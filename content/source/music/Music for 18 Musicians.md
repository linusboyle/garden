> _**Music for 18 Musicians**_ is a work of [minimalist music](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Minimal_music "Minimal music") composed by [Steve Reich](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Steve_Reich "Steve Reich") during 1974–1976. Its world premiere was on April 24, 1976, at [The Town Hall](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Town_Hall_(New_York_City) "The Town Hall (New York City)") in the [Midtown Manhattan](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Midtown_Manhattan "Midtown Manhattan") [Theater District](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Theater_District,_Manhattan "Theater District, Manhattan"). Following this, [a recording of the piece](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Music_for_18_Musicians_(album) "Music for 18 Musicians (album)") was released on the [ECM New Series](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/ECM_(record_label) "ECM (record label)") in 1978.

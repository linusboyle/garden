# I am Embarrassed


Created: [[2025-05-21]] 

> A dividing line that was circulated quite early on was the idea that whereas OSR gamers were wedded to modern releases being tonally and mechanically compatible with TSR-era D&D, NSR gamers were happier retaining a more nebulous commitment to ‘old school’ sensibilities and using these to fuel the creation of both weird settings and mechanics that were considerably lighter than anything previously associated with D&D.

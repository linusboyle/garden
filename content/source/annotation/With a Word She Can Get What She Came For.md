# With a Word She Can Get What She Came For


Created: [[2025-05-05]] 

> 未成年的少女们在歌曲中被凝固为色欲的对象，乐手肆意勾勒她们性感的姿态，向她们调情引诱，但歌曲中永远听不到她们的回答。从这种意义上，“baby groupies”的确是the Muse of Rock & Roll，和酒精、毒品等物件一起，催生了rock stars们灵光乍现的“mystical experiences”：But these women were more than just the sex that went with the drugs and the rock and roll. They were muses. They inspired songs. They raced through the minds of famous musicians as those stars strummed out the first few notes of a future classic.

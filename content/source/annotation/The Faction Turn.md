# The Faction Turn


Created: [[2025-01-31]] 

> A fun thing to do is to assign a faction NPC to be in charge of each faction goal. I call this person a lieutenant. Even more fun if they have a personal desire that runs a bit counter to the goal sometimes, giving the PCs an opportunity to influence the lieutenant.

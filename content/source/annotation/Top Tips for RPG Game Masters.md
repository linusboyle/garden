# Top Tips for RPG Game Masters


Created: [[2025-05-20]] 

> Don't build stories during prep. Watch them unfold at the table. Bring the world, the situation, the quests, and the non-player characters to the table and watch and react as the characters crash into them.



[See in context](https://hyp.is/FofJUDUrEfC8iAOV6nzdAw/slyflourish.com/top_advice.html) at [Top Tips for RPG Game Masters](https://slyflourish.com/top_advice.html)



Created: [[2025-05-20]] 

> Instead of developing plots with directions we expect the characters to follow, develop situations in which the characters get involved.



[See in context](https://hyp.is/HDq7tDUrEfCazEcVMEZetQ/slyflourish.com/top_advice.html) at [Top Tips for RPG Game Masters](https://slyflourish.com/top_advice.html)



Created: [[2025-05-20]]

Created: [[2025-05-20]] 

> For online play, use the tools and virtual tabletops that most easily support an evolving game

Created: [[2025-05-20]] 

> You may have big ideas for a multi-year campaign but the only game to worry about is the next one you're going to run.



[See in context](https://hyp.is/hClNqDUrEfCki4NBXqfS7Q/slyflourish.com/top_advice.html) at [Top Tips for RPG Game Masters](https://slyflourish.com/top_advice.html)



Created: [[2025-05-20]] 

> When developing your own campaign or game world, instead of starting with a full pantheon of gods, histories of the kingdom, and your own planes of existence, focus on what matters to the characters in the next session you run.

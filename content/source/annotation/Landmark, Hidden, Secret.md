Created: [[2026-03-05]] 

> Whether players can even learn the existence of secret information is something I think judges disagree about



[See in context](https://hyp.is/FHPnnBhmEfG2JocZK9tMcg/diyanddragons.blogspot.com/2019/10/landmark-hidden-secret.html) at [Landmark, Hidden, Secret](https://diyanddragons.blogspot.com/2019/10/landmark-hidden-secret.html)



Created: [[2026-03-05]] 

> I would say that the existence of a secret should be hidden information. I would say that players should be able to prove there is a secret by asking a question and taking a risk. Actually learning the secret should require rolling the dice, but discovering that there IS a secret there to be learned should not be a secret unto itself.



[See in context](https://hyp.is/HNqkZhhmEfGwkN9TQ69REw/diyanddragons.blogspot.com/2019/10/landmark-hidden-secret.html) at [Landmark, Hidden, Secret](https://diyanddragons.blogspot.com/2019/10/landmark-hidden-secret.html)



Created: [[2026-03-05]] 

> You discover hidden information by examining landmarks. You learn secrets by examining hidden information.



[See in context](https://hyp.is/M7HBOBhmEfGsuzdth6iMvA/diyanddragons.blogspot.com/2019/10/landmark-hidden-secret.html) at [Landmark, Hidden, Secret](https://diyanddragons.blogspot.com/2019/10/landmark-hidden-secret.html)



Created: [[2026-03-05]]

[See in context](https://hyp.is/lbIyFBhmEfGKTyMpb69poQ/diyanddragons.blogspot.com/2019/10/landmark-hidden-secret.html) at [Landmark, Hidden, Secret](https://diyanddragons.blogspot.com/2019/10/landmark-hidden-secret.html)



Created: [[2026-03-05]] 

> The difference between landmark information and hidden information isn't just the difference between what you say when players first enter a room and what they have to ask you to find out. It's also the difference between information that is free and information that comes at a cost.



[See in context](https://hyp.is/DPFgjhhnEfGQaMcKxK5flA/diyanddragons.blogspot.com/2019/10/landmark-hidden-secret.html) at [Landmark, Hidden, Secret](https://diyanddragons.blogspot.com/2019/10/landmark-hidden-secret.html)



Created: [[2026-03-05]] 

> Something can become secret simply because there's not enough time to find it.



[See in context](https://hyp.is/GAgAJhhoEfGYQSfieszv2A/diyanddragons.blogspot.com/2019/10/landmark-hidden-secret.html) at [Landmark, Hidden, Secret](https://diyanddragons.blogspot.com/2019/10/landmark-hidden-secret.html)



Created: [[2026-03-05]] 

> For there to be not enough time there has to be some kind of time pressure, some kind of countdown or deadline, either narrative or mechanical, some kind of reason the characters can't just spend all day. Remember, for the players, that search only takes as long as they need to say they agree to it.



[See in context](https://hyp.is/UCLRUhhoEfGb9kOMrP4iQw/diyanddragons.blogspot.com/2019/10/landmark-hidden-secret.html) at [Landmark, Hidden, Secret](https://diyanddragons.blogspot.com/2019/10/landmark-hidden-secret.html)



Created: [[2026-03-05]] 

> Knowing how to solve a problem like that quickly is a skill. It's special knowledge that not everyone - not every character - possesses. And even if a character has the right skill, there's still a chance that they'll fail. So roll the dice. Roll the dice, because the alternative is to make the players try to act out their characters' searches. Roll the dice because while the characters might have all day, the players don't, and their time, your time, is worth more than trying to devise and explain the correct search algorithm.



[See in context](https://hyp.is/h80FoBhoEfG3yAcAgrNpsQ/diyanddragons.blogspot.com/2019/10/landmark-hidden-secret.html) at [Landmark, Hidden, Secret](https://diyanddragons.blogspot.com/2019/10/landmark-hidden-secret.html)



Created: [[2026-03-05]] 

> Having to explicitly request to look closer is already a barrier to discovery. The additional barrier of a dice roll is appropriate in some situations, but not every situation



[See in context](https://hyp.is/xF1_XhhoEfG_encn0U2u4A/diyanddragons.blogspot.com/2019/10/landmark-hidden-secret.html) at [Landmark, Hidden, Secret](https://diyanddragons.blogspot.com/2019/10/landmark-hidden-secret.html)

> Information can safely be made secret under one of two conditions. The first is if that information is truly optional

# The Basis of the Game is Making Decisions


Created: [[2025-05-30]] 

> The core activity of roleplaying games is making decisions. Every other activity feeds back into this core in one way or another. Rules are there to control and shape the field of possible decisions, determine the likelihood of particular outcomes of those decisions, and to structure who makes which decision.



[See in context](https://hyp.is/5USZQj1mEfCHoTNz557hcw/retiredadventurer.blogspot.com/2019/01/the-basis-of-game-is-making-decisions.html) at [The Basis of the Game is Making Decisions](https://retiredadventurer.blogspot.com/2019/01/the-basis-of-game-is-making-decisions.html)

# linear set
> A subset of ${\displaystyle \mathbb {N} ^{d}}$ is said to be **linear** if it is of the form
> 
> ${\displaystyle \left\{\mathbf {v} +\sum _{i=1}^{m}k_{i}\mathbf {v} _{i}\,\colon \,k_{1},\dots ,k_{m}\in \mathbb {N} \right\}}$
> 
> where ${\displaystyle m}$ is some [integer](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Integer) and ${\displaystyle \mathbf {v} ,\mathbf {v} _{1},\dots ,\mathbf {v} _{m}}$ are fixed vectors in ${\displaystyle \mathbb {N} ^{d}}$. 

也叫做**finite generalized arithmetic progression**, or sometimes just **generalized arithmetic progression** (GAP)，因为可以视作等差数列的一个扩展：${\displaystyle \mathbf {v} ,\mathbf {v} _{1},\dots ,\mathbf {v} _{m}}$是差，而数列中的数用m个index $k_1,\cdots,k_m$来访问。

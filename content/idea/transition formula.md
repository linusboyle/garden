A transition formula's free variables range over $V \cup V'$ (as opposed to a [[state formula]]).

It denotes a set of transitions $(s, s')$, with $(s, s') \models \varphi$ iff $\sigma \models \varphi$ where $\sigma(v) = s(v)$ for v in V and $\sigma(v) = s'(v)$ for v in V'

> What if the number of unwindings specified is too small? In this case, bugs that require paths that are deeper may be missed. In order to address this problem, CBMC can optionally insert checks that the given unwinding bound is actually sufficiently large. These checks are called _unwinding assertions_, and are enabled with the option `--unwinding-assertions`.

# weakest precondition

$wp([[S]],\phi)$ stands for **weakest precondition** of $\phi$ under program S.

It's stronger than [[weakest liberal precondition|weakest liberal precondition]], and guarantees termination.

However, we often refer to wlp when we talk about weakest precondition.

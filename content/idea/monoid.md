# 幺半群 monoid

A **monoid** is a triple $(X, \Box, i)$ consisting of a set _X_, a binary operation □, and a special element $i \in X$ (called the **identity element** of _X_), satisfying these rules:

(associative law)

$(x \Box y) \Box z=x\Box (y \Box z)$

(identity law)

$x \Box i=x=i \Box x$

for all $x, y, z \in X$.

A monoid is a [[semigroup]] with an identity element.

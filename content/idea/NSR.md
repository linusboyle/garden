一种在 [[OSR]] 基础上发展而来的[[桌面角色扮演游戏]]设计风格，用来描述与 OSR 游戏有相近的原则或哲学，但在机制上老式 DnD 并不兼容。

代表作品是：

- Into the Odds
- Troika
- World of Dungeons
- Mothership
- Cairn

最初在一篇博客中提出：[New School Revolution – The Bone Box Chant](https://boneboxchant.wordpress.com/2019/12/21/nsr/)，其中总结了其认为的若干 NSR 特点。后来主要由 Yochai Gal 对 NSR 的概念和社群进行发展。值得注意的是，在这篇 2022 年的[博文](https://newschoolrevolution.com/the-new-new-school-revolution/)中，Yochai 试图借 NSR 这一名词的提出摆脱 OSR 社群中存在的右翼影响：

>  The OSR/Indie TTRPG community is full of bad actors. Identifying and tracking these folks so that they and their followers do not gain an active presence in our communities is pivotal.

[Taskland](https://tasker.land/2025/05/21/i-am-embarrassed/) 对此评论道：

>  Once a series of indirectly-compatible games with a discernible ‘old school’ sensibilities made it successfully across the minefield, NSR games and their creators were re-absorbed into the scene that had once sought to keep them at arms’ length. With the OSR no longer pushing quite so hard, NSR people **a)** stopped pulling away and **b)** went very quiet about the need to keep policing their social boundaries.
>  
>  By the time Yochai Gal was getting hounded off of Bluesky for sharing a link to someone with truly depressing politics, the distinction between NSR and OSR was nothing but a historical footnote: If you were engaged with the NSR, you were engaged with the OSR… _No daylight, kids_.
